Guidelines

Accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA)

Rules of engagement for inclusive design.

The "Do's"

  • Ensure Contrast: Maintain a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 for normal text. Onyx on Porcelain is a perfect 17.5:1.

  • Focus States: Interactive elements must have a visible focus ring. For dark buttons, use a light focus ring with offset, and vice-versa.

  • Label Forms: Use persistent, visible labels for all inputs. Placeholders are not replacements for labels.

The "Don'ts"

  • Never use white text on Lemon Lime. The contrast ratio is only 1.2:1 (Fails). Always use Onyx or Shadow Grey text on Lemon Lime.

  • Don't rely solely on color. Error states must include text explanations or icons, not just a red border.

  • Avoid Olive Grey for body text. It is too light for long reading and only passes on very dark backgrounds. Use Shadow Grey instead.
